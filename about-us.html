Why We Started

Doctors spend up to 50% of their time on clinical documentation — writing notes, transcribing consultations, filling out records. We saw healthcare professionals burning out on paperwork instead of focusing on the patients in front of them.

MedConverse AI was born to change that — using real-time transcription, speaker diarization, and AI-powered clinical analysis to turn every consultation into structured, accurate medical notes automatically.

Doctor First

Every feature — from real-time transcription to AI summaries — starts with one question: does it give doctors more time with patients?

Trust & Security

Innovation
